1. Read a problem 
2. Set a 20(for medium) - 30(for hard) minute timer
	2.1 10-20 minutes to brainstorm for the approach
	2.2 10 minutes to come up with the code.
3. Come up with the best method you can within the that time
4. In case of failure, find the BKMs(Best Known Methods). 
5. Even after finding the BKM, dry run it. Understand why it works.
6. Again, try the logic yourself. Verify with the golden logic until you make it work.
7. Repeat
